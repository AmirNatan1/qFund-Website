HOW QFUND EVALUATES DEEP TECH

Founders. Technology. Market. Defensibility.

01

Founders. Domain authority with deep, proven expertise. The founder must be the world’s leading expert on this specific problem.

02

Technology. 10× performance improvement in a critical dimension. Proprietary, defensible, and not replicable for two to three years.

03

Market. Massive markets undergoing transformation. A real-world problem with a clear path to market dominance.

04

Defensibility. Strong IP position: patents, trade secrets, and years of hard R&D. A technology barrier that compounds over time.
