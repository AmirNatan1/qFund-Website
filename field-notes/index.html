Active signals

Signals become useful when they change where you look.

We follow shifts in technical capability, system constraints, and market readiness. The overlap—not the headline—is often where a new company can form.

Q/01WORKING THESIS

Quantum

Quantum utility arrives before fault tolerance

The most valuable near-term systems may be those that make imperfect hardware measurably more useful, not those that wait for perfection.

Signals to watch

Error suppression · orchestration · domain-specific advantage

As compute density rises, cooling and power delivery move from facilities constraints to core product architecture.
